WEEKLY FLORAL WORKSHOPS

Every Wednesday from 18:30 PM

Join the team at Bloomerang for an evening of craft and creativity!

All materials will be provided including a selection of seasonal flowers and a vase for you to keep. We will also have fresh juices and healthy treats.



We run floristry workshops on a seasonal basis. Our Floristry classes are for people at all levels. We welcome beginners. We introduce you to floristry in a relaxed way and it is great fun. Brighten up your home with a new creation made by you in floristry class. We have received great feedback from these workshops and many participants have become regulars.

Spring/autumn workshop - how to make your own wreath for spring/Easter autumn/Halloween using seasonal flowers and materials available in our shop

Christmas workshop - how to make a table center piece for the holiday festivities, and you will learn how to design a table-scape using berries, foliage and all the red flowers available in stock.



We also run themed workshops, for those who want to release their creativity beyond the season boundary.

Hand-tied bouquet workshop - This class starts with an introduction on how to condition flowers. You'll learn how to bring out the most striking elements of the natural materials you use. Throughout the course, we'll demonstrate the classic spiral technique, look at colour matching, and choosing flowers for two distinct arrangements: the natural style bouquet and the dome style bouquet.

Ikebana - on this one day workshop we you will discover the philosophy of the century old craft, the vessels used and the different techniques and principles.

On all of our courses, we encourage students to experiment with the skills and themes of traditional and contemporary flower arranging, developing their own creative vision under the guidance of experienced tutors.
